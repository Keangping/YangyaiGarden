About

Yangyai Garden, the name came from the biggest tree on Koh Phangan, combined with a garden on our place which is grown with coconut tree, fruit, vegetable and herb. Our garden is organic and has a certificate. Guests can enjoy our products from the garden as well. Our philosophy of service is sincere, friendly and honest.
