Full Moon Party

Description

The Full Moon Party is an all-night beach party that originated in Hat Rin on the island of Ko Pha-ngan, Thailand on the night of, before or after every full moon. It is mostly attended by tourists. The type of music mainly is electronic.

Jungle Experience

Description

Jungle Experience is one of the original underground dance gatherings. This unique eco party location in a beautiful secret garden deep in the Baan Tai jungle has a natural mountain stream running through the party leading you to the stunningly decorated dancefloor.UNDERGROUND house & techno mixed by Jungle's resident DJ's & regular international guests.With dancers & performance artists we promise you a truly unique party experience which will stay in your heart forever.

Waterfall Party

Description

One of Koh Phangan's iconic 'big 3' parties, 'Waterfall Party' has quickly come from humble roots, to one of the largest organized music events on Asia's 'Techno Island'. Think international DJ's, Fire Shows, Dancers, Acrobats, Glow Paint and a dance floor that doesn't stop until you do! All this set around a beautiful waterfall in the heart of a tropical jungle.

Schedules
  • 2 day before & after Fullmoon party

Half Moon Festival

Description

The Half Moon Festival is an outdoor dance music (rave) event on the island of Ko Pha Ngan, Thailand held on the half moon, one week before and one week after the full moon. The Half Moon Festival complements the bigger Full Moon Party events. It is held in its own venue in a forest north of the Ban Tai village, between Thong Sala and Haad Rin.

Schedules
  • 15, 29 December 2018
  • 12, 28 January 2019

  • 12, 27 April 2019
  • 11, 26 May 2019

Blackmoon Culture

Description

Peace, Trance, Dance ...these 3 words are the main motivation behind the famous Blackmoon Culture. Every month at the black moon, we all gather on the sandy beach at Mac's Bay for a night filled with the latest and freshest progressive and psychedelic trance brought by our Thai and International residents and guest dj's, while the Magic Phangan family and Carin Dickson serve breath-taking eye candy with their decoration.
